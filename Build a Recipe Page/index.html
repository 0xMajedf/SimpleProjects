Cooking a Burger

Burger is some kind of food with some ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Meat
  • Lettuce

Instructions

  1. First, take two pieces of Italian bread.
  2. Add a slice of cooked beef (cow meat) on the bread.
  3. Add a tomato slice.
  4. Add some lettuce.
  5. Lastly, add sauce and place the second bread on top.
